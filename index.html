Who I am

Archaeologist, filmmaker, and storyteller based in York, UK.

Portrait photo

SITE: DN-PORTFOLIO-2024
RECORDER: D. Naresh
LAYER: Base deposit — biography

I'm a multimedia producer and archaeologist with a passion for making the ancient world viscerally present — through film, immersive technology, and the still image.

My work sits at the boundary between rigorous research and experiential storytelling. Currently I lead research at Visualising Heritage, University of Bradford, exploring innovative approaches to heritage communication — from 360° film and VR to projection mapping and photogrammetry. Previously, I produced documentary content for CNN and National Geographic, telling character-driven stories with global reach.
